My Hackathon Project

Earlier this year, in February, I attended my first Hackathon hosted by Symantec in Mountain View. Here is the project that we worked on that day.

You can visit the website we made here.

The theme of the Hackathon was to produce a project related to either civil rights or human rights. My group chose to go down the path of civil rights. Our website aimed to educate the public about the Constitution of the United States of America.




This is the side-scrolling game that I made last week. The goal is to help the sprite save the environment by collecting all of the plastic water bottles and to avoid the clouds of carbon dioxide by jumping over them.
